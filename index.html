PLAY

Your music companion

The "Play" feature in a music app typically allows users to start playback of a selected song or playlist. The user can tap on the "Play" button within the app to begin listening to the music. Once the music starts playing, the app may also display additional playback controls such as play/pause, skip forward/backwards, and volume controls. The "Play" feature is essential to any music app, as it allows users to enjoy their favourite songs and playlists with ease.

ABOUT US

Your music companion

Telma and I were inspired to create a music app that offered a unique user experience and solved common problems that we had encountered with other music apps.

Our motivation stemmed from our passion for music and technology, as well as our desire to showcase our programming skills and pursue careers in app development. Throughout the development process, we applied our collective knowledge of programming languages, frameworks, and software development processes to create a well-designed app that people would enjoy using.

As a portfolio project for Holberton School, our music app demonstrated our proficiency in various programming languages and showcased our ability to create a user-friendly interface, optimize performance, and integrate with external APIs.

If you're interested, you can check out our LinkedIn, GitHub, and Twitter profiles for more information about our project and team members.
